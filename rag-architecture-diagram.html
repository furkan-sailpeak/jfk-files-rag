Why hybrid retrieval?
FTS is strong on proper nouns but misses paraphrase ("who killed Oswald" ↛ "Ruby fired the fatal shot"). The vector leg recovers semantic neighbours; FTS keeps precision on named entities.
Three post-generation guards
Grounding → Expansion retry → Citation verifier. Any claim without real source support is caught or stripped before it reaches the user. Critical for forensic use.
Evaluation
30 corpus-grounded questions (eval/). Judged by gpt-5.4-mini against reference answers authored from the archive. Benchmarked head-to-head against plain GPT-5.4 with no retrieval.
